About

As consumer demand diminishes due to the COVID-19 pandemic and as America faces 30 million lost jobs, it's vital as ever to get food in the hands of those that need it. We connect food banks with food processors to ensure that America’s food doesn’t go to waste as families go hungry.

Breadbasket is a volunteer initiative to efficiently allocate food where it's needed in America.
