Breadbasket is a volunteer initiative that efficiently allocates food to food banks in America. As consumer demand diminishes due to the COVID-19 pandemic and as America faces a record unemployment rate, it's vital to get food in the hands of those that need it most. We connect food banks with food processors to ensure that America’s food doesn’t go to waste as families go hungry.
